enum rm_zmod4xxx_event_t

Event in the callback function

Enumerator
RM_ZMOD4XXX_EVENT_SUCCESS 

Operation was completed successfully.

RM_ZMOD4XXX_EVENT_MEASUREMENT_COMPLETE 

Sensor measurement results read successfully.

RM_ZMOD4XXX_EVENT_MEASUREMENT_NOT_COMPLETE 

Sensor measurement in progress.

RM_ZMOD4XXX_EVENT_DEV_ERR_POWER_ON_RESET 

Unexpected reset.

RM_ZMOD4XXX_EVENT_DEV_ERR_ACCESS_CONFLICT 

Getting invalid results while results readout.

RM_ZMOD4XXX_EVENT_DEV_ERR_DAMAGE 

Sensor may be damaged.

RM_ZMOD4XXX_EVENT_ERROR 

A communication error has occurred.
